About the Maker

What began as a quiet summer curiosity at just ten years old has blossomed into a vibrant journey of creativity. A needle, some thread, and a spark of imagination ignited a passion for sewing that simply couldn't be contained. Now, Calla Coastal is the proud vision of a dedicated 13-year-old artisan. Each bag is lovingly handcrafted, transforming from a simple piece of fabric into a traveling companion that has already found its way to corners of the world far beyond the sewing room where it was born.
